About Our University

Attendance at the University of Students is a privilege. Should you be selected, you will be guaranteed a job in your field after graduation, courtesy of our close ties with recruitment agencies and the extremely high quality of our globally-recognised education.

Founded with a vision of accessible, excellent education, we welcome students from all walks of life to join a community that thrives on curiosity, collaboration, and courage.

Our Mission

The University of Students exists to advance knowledge and learning for the benefit of society. We believe every student deserves access to world-class education, mentorship, and the opportunity to grow into a leader in their field.

Founded in 1912, we have grown from a regional college into one of Australia's most respected research-intensive universities — while never losing sight of our founding commitment to community.
