Breakthroughs in Microbiology

Scientists at the University of Students have discovered Chronos mutabilis, a bacterium that rewrites its own DNA in real-time. This organism adapts to extreme environments within milliseconds, defying the known laws of cellular decay.

This breakthrough opens the door to programmable, regenerative life forms that could soon redefine modern medicine.
