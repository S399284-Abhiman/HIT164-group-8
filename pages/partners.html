How We Work Together

Multiple pathways to suit your organisation's goals

  • Contract ResearchCommission targeted research and consulting from our specialist teams to solve specific business or policy challenges.
  • Global PartnershipsJoin our international network of universities and organisations to tackle global challenges through cross-border collaboration.
  • Sponsorships & EventsSponsor UoS events, competitions, and programs to connect your brand with our community of students, researchers, and alumni.
  • Curriculum Co-designWork with our academic teams to co-design industry-relevant curricula, guest lecture programs, and capstone projects.
  • Clinical & Field TrialsPartner with our health and science faculties to run clinical research and access patient-centred research infrastructure.

Research Collaboration

UoS Australia offers several research collaboration models depending on your goals and investment level.

  • ARC Linkage GrantsCo-funded government grants for industry-academic research. We can help you apply.
  • Industry-Funded ProjectsCommission a research team directly. IP arrangements are negotiated upfront.
  • CRC SchemesJoin a Cooperative Research Centre to tackle long-term industry challenges with multiple partners.

Student Placements

Hosting a UoS student placement is straightforward. Here's how it works:

  • Step 1 — RegisterSubmit a placement opportunity through our Work Integrated Learning office.
  • Step 2 — MatchingWe match you with students from relevant programs based on skills and project fit.
  • Step 3 — OnboardWe handle the agreements. You focus on giving students a meaningful experience.

Philanthropic Giving

Your investment in UoS Australia creates lasting impact. Giving options include:

  • Student ScholarshipsFund access to education for talented students who need financial support.
  • Research ChairsEndow a named professorship in an area aligned with your organisation's mission.
  • Facility & EquipmentFund labs, equipment, or spaces that bear your organisation's name for decades.

All gifts to UoS Australia are tax-deductible under the Australian Tax Act.
