Our Mission

Advancing Knowledge, Empowering People

The University of S Australia is a dynamic, student-centred institution dedicated to delivering world-class education and transformative research outcomes. Founded on principles of inclusion, innovation, and integrity, we serve a diverse community of learners across every discipline.

Our campuses connect local ambition with global opportunity — preparing graduates not just for the careers of today, but the challenges of tomorrow.

Reconciliation

Our Commitment to First Nations Peoples

UoS Australia acknowledges the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples as the Traditional Custodians of the lands and waters on which our campuses are situated. We pay our respects to Elders past, present, and emerging.

Our Reconciliation Action Plan sets out our commitment to building relationships, respect, and opportunities for First Nations students, staff, and communities. We are dedicated to closing the gap in education outcomes and to embedding Indigenous perspectives across our curriculum and research.
